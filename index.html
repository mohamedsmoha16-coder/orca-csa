How long does it take to set up?▾

Most customers are fully set up and live within 10–15 minutes. Just connect your channels, upload your FAQs or product info, and ORCA starts responding immediately. No coding required.

What if ORCA can't answer a question?▾

ORCA automatically escalates complex or unknown queries to your human team with full conversation context. Your customers never hit a dead end — and you stay in control.

Is my customer data secure?▾

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We're GDPR-compliant and never share your customer data with third parties. Your data is yours — always.

Can I cancel anytime?▾

Absolutely. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. We believe you stay because it works — not because you're locked in.

What languages does ORCA support?▾

ORCA automatically detects and responds in the customer's language. It supports 50+ languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, and more — no extra setup required.
